Wardrobe

What to wear for your Japan trip

Seasonal Clothing

In the summer, lightweight and breathable clothing is ideal, while in the winter, pack warm clothes like jackets and scarves to stay cozy.

Traditional Wear

Consider purchasing a Yukata or Kimono for special occasions or for wearing during festivals in Japan.

Comfortable Footwear

Since you'll be walking a lot, it's important to pack comfortable shoes that can handle both city streets and nature trails.
