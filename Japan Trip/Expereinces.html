Experiences

Unforgettable moments in Japan

Tea Ceremony

Participate in a traditional Japanese tea ceremony and experience the culture and etiquette behind the art of tea-making.

Visit Mount Fuji

Climb or take a scenic tour around Mount Fuji, Japan's tallest peak, and enjoy its beauty from various viewpoints.

Cherry Blossom Viewing

Experience the famous cherry blossom season (Sakura) in Japan during spring, when parks are filled with stunning pink blooms.
