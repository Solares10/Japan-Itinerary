Travel Tips

Essential advice for your trip to Japan

Best Time to Visit Japan

The best time to visit Japan is during the spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November), as the weather is pleasant and the scenery is breathtaking.

Language Tips

Although many people in Japan understand English, it's helpful to know basic Japanese phrases such as "Hello" (こんにちは), "Thank you" (ありがとうございます), and "Goodbye" (さようなら).

Money & Currency

Japan is a cash-based society, so it's advisable to carry cash or use a prepaid travel card. However, credit cards are widely accepted in major cities.
